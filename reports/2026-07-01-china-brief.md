Sits against DoD's >600 (mid-2024) → **1,000+ warheads by 2030** projection. *Survivable road-mobile intercontinental strike expansion reshapes the strategic backdrop India reads through its credible-minimum-deterrence lens.* (china-arms, SCMP [403], Wikipedia/DF-61, Cosmo Science)

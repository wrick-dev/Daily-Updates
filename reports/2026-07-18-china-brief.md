## 🇮🇳 India Watch — Net Assessment

**Today's signal is institutional, not kinetic.** July 18 is a genuinely significant day, but the significance is in **AI-governance architecture**, not the LAC or the IOR. Beijing used its flagship AI set-piece to (a) stand up **WAICO**, a Shanghai-seated rules body with **Pakistan inside and India outside**; (b) have **Xi personally pitch China as the Global South's AI partner** (5,000 training slots); and (c) **display the Atlas-950 hardware** as proof the domestic stack can run without US silicon. **What to watch next (72h):** (1) any **MEA line on WAICO / India's non-participation**, and whether India signals toward Pax Silica / GPAI; (2) **July-19 Brahmaputra-dam** anniversary messaging from Beijing; (3) **DeepSeek V4 official release** (legacy retire after July 24) — possible WAIC-window reveal; (4) **WAIC Day 3–4** (July 19–20) for further Ascend/chip and governance detail; (5) the **Joint Sea-2026 Pacific patrol's** declared scope/location.

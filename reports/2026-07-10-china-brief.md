## 🇮🇳 India Watch — Net Assessment

For India the strategic read is unchanged and reinforced: a **confident, demonstrated sea leg of China's triad**, where the same JL-3 that ranges the continental US covers the entire subcontinent from protected near-China bastions — compressing warning times, pressuring India's still-maturing **Arihant-class SSBN** deterrent, and underscoring the case for the in-development **K-6 SLBM**. Two **new PLA signalling items** thicken the picture: a **CASI report on new mass-salvo multi-cell missile launchers at Jilantai** (a PLARF proving ground — a leading indicator for force-wide fires proliferation) and **CCTV's first-ever DF-17 hypersonic launch footage (July 8)** as a deliberate show of force. On the bilateral track the **25th SR round** stays agreed-for-China-2026 but undated; the **Medog mega-dam** and the **Pakistan J-35 package** remain the unresolved hard files — the thaw remains **tactical, not strategic realignment**. **Key watch items (mid-July):** (1) any **China reaction to the Melbourne uranium/space deliverables**; (2) whether a **second SLBM launch or an SSBN Pacific patrol** follows the July-6 test; (3) confirmation (or continued absence) of the **Jiu Tian operational mission**; (4) whether the **July-13 Han Kuang window** triggers a **PLA Eastern-Theater surge**; (5) Huawei's **WAIC-2026** Ascend/Atlas-950 announcements and any DeepSeek-chip detail (July 17–20); (6) a firm **date for the 25th SR round**; (7) any spread of the **Jilantai multi-cell launcher** concept to operational garrisons.

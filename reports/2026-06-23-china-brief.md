## 1. Defense Tech & Platforms

- **[NEW 06-23] First public DF-17 launch footage [SIGNIFICANT].** CCTV (June 20–21) released the first-ever public clip of a DF-17 hypersonic glide vehicle launching from a TEL in the Gobi, during a multi-service joint drill featuring "multiple waves of simulated strikes" under strong electromagnetic interference. Commentator Du Wenlong framed it as a Rocket Force combat-readiness statement; the DF-17 (~1,800–2,500 km) ranges the SCS and US bases in Japan. *Implication: a deliberate anti-carrier/A2AD signaling cadence — the regional strike environment India factors into Indo-Pacific and northern-border planning.* (Global Times, Zona Militar, SCMP, Newsweek — June 20–22)
- **J-36 / J-50 sixth-gen testing intensifies; US accelerates F-47 in response (carried) [SIGNIFICANT].** A new DoW/Pentagon report confirms China flying two sixth-gen prototypes — the Chengdu **J-36** (tailless, tri-engine, ~45–54 t, land-based air superiority) and Shenyang **J-50** (carrier-capable, twin-engine, folding wings). Operational capability projected mid-2030s; the USAF FY27 request jumped ~65% to ~$5.03B for the Boeing F-47 in explicit reaction. *Implication: a maturing PLAAF/PLAN sixth-gen track widening the long-term air-power balance India must price in.* (Army Recognition, 19FortyFive, Flight Global — June 2026)
- **GDF-600 hypersonic boost-glide vehicle (carried).** The Guangdong Aerodynamic Research Academy's GDF-600 can release sub-munitions (missiles/drones) mid-flight and is adaptable to air and naval platforms. *Implication: a modular, multi-domain hypersonic strike concept broadening China's standoff options.* (The Defence News, Army Recognition)
- **Fujian (Type 003) → FOC targeted 2026 (context).** The 80,000-ton EMALS carrier (commissioned Nov 2025) is moving from near-coastal training toward extended Western Pacific deployments; first to catapult-launch the J-35. *Implication: a third, far more capable carrier raising the ceiling for future IOR deployments.* (USNI [403], Defence Security Asia)

- **J-6W drone-conversion mass near Taiwan (context).** Hundreds of potential drone-converted J-6 airframes positioned for air-defense saturation. *Implication: a cheap-mass attrition concept India tracks for swarm-defense planning.* (Army Recognition)

## 3. AI / Semiconductors

- **DeepSeek V4-Pro on Huawei Ascend 950 — fully domestic stack (carried, June 19) [SIGNIFICANT].** The 1.6-trillion-parameter MoE was trained with no Nvidia silicon; ~1,000 Ascend 910C chips used for post-training, with each 910C ~60% of an H100 for inference. SMIC (fab) → Huawei Ascend (accelerators) → DeepSeek (models) now forms a credible sanctions-resilient stack, reshaping AI-infrastructure choices across the Global South. *Implication: erodes US export-control leverage and offers India's neighborhood a non-Western AI option to weigh.* (Fortune, Tom's Hardware, CSIS — Apr–Jun 2026)
- **US escalates IP-theft accusations against DeepSeek (context).** Washington alleges IP theft by DeepSeek and other Chinese AI firms as V4 ships. *Implication: the US–China AI rivalry hardening into a legal/export-control front India must navigate as a swing market.* (Tom's Hardware)
- **15th Five-Year Plan targets quantum, AI, 6G, bio-manufacturing, embodied intelligence (context).** Drafting centers "new quality productive forces" and a future-industries investment-growth mechanism. *Implication: continuity of the tech-self-reliance drive — a competitive backdrop to India's manufacturing/semiconductor push.* (The Quantum Insider, CGTN)

## 5. Space

- **LM-12B reusable booster debut; no recovery attempt (context).** The new vehicle flew paying Qianfan customers; the landing-capable booster made no recovery attempt on this flight. *Implication: incremental reusable-launch progress, still short of operational recovery — a cadence/cost race India tracks.* (NASASpaceFlight, china-in-space.com)

## 🇮🇳 India Watch — Net Assessment

The cycle closes on a **two-track signal**. On the diplomatic track, the **Wang Yi–Doval readout** makes the "gradual normalisation" framing official, layered with tangible deliverables — rare-earth magnet resumption, Kailash Mansarovar Yatra, direct-flight/ASA talks — while India uses its **BRICS chair** to drive the counter-terror + ICT-security agenda and press Pakistan-based terrorism. Crucially, this is relationship-management, **not** a boundary breakthrough: no SR round was announced, and the counterweights remain live — the named **Mustang Panda campaign against Indian banks** (plus ports/rail/power targeting), the unresolved **Brahmaputra mega-dam**, and tightening China–Pakistan ties. On the hard-power track the build continues regardless of the Delhi mood music: **first public DF-17 hypersonic footage** as a deliberate A2AD signal, **J-36/J-50** forcing a visible US F-47 budget reaction, **DeepSeek V4 on Ascend** hardening a sanctions-resilient AI stack, dual **Qianfan/Guowang** LEO mass (Qianfan ~200), and **quantum moving into industrial application**. Backdrop risk: the **hollowed PLA command structure** keeps miscalculation/readiness questions on the board opposite the LAC. Net: a genuine thaw worth banking the deliverables from — pursued with clear eyes about the capability gap and the parallel pressure China keeps applying.
